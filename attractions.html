Vidhana Soudha is an impressive landmark in Bengaluru, serving as the seat of the state legislature of Karnataka. Built in the Dravidian style, this magnificent granite building is a fine example of post-independence Indian architecture, blending traditional and modern elements. Its grand, symmetrical structure and imposing dome make it a powerful symbol of the state's governance. The building is particularly beautiful at night when it's illuminated, attracting many visitors who come to admire its majestic presence.
