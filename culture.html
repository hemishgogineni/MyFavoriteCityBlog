Discover Bengaluru’s Rich Culture

Traditional Food

Bengaluru is known for its diverse culinary culture — from iconic masala dosas and idlis to spicy chaat and fusion cuisines. The city blends Karnataka’s traditional flavors with influences from across India and beyond.

Festivals

The city celebrates a variety of festivals like Ugadi, Diwali, Karaga, and Ganesh Chaturthi with grandeur. These occasions are marked by colorful decorations, processions, music, and community gatherings.

Music & Dance

Bengaluru is home to both classical Carnatic concerts and a vibrant indie music scene. Traditional dance forms like Bharatanatyam thrive alongside modern performances and cultural fests.

Art & Literature

From art galleries and street murals to literary fests and theatre, Bengaluru’s creative spirit is evident everywhere. Spaces like Rangashankara and NGMA host regular exhibitions and plays.
